The Alien's Day Off

Thomas, the star commander of his alien army finally gets a day off! And good thing too, because Thomas didn't think he could take anymore of the gross alien food. Thomas is vegan and the choices they had were very limited, some making him turn more green than you can imagine. While Thomas was enjoying his day off he noticed another person. Was it..."Bobby?". Thomas was beyond confused, where did bobby come from, why is he here, and why is there A PLANET BY HIM????
